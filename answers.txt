If songs.db contains the top 100 songs of one listener from 2018, how would you characterize their audio aura?
    If I wanted to charactorize their audio aura I would use queries to get the averages of the energy, valence,
    and danceability of a person’s top 100 songs. I would then create ranges of numbers for each "aura" and whichever
    range the person's averages are closest to would be their audio aura.

Hypothesize about why the way you’ve calculated this aura might not be very representative of the listener. What better ways of calculating this aura would you propose?
    The way I calculated it using averages can exclude major factors, such as scenarios where the person listens to both high and low energy music, since that would
    average to a moderate level energy which is inaccurate. A better way of calculating could be to use cluster-ish grouping or look at ranges instead so that averages
    don't blend the statistics.
